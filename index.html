Summary

Zendaya Coleman is a model, actress, and singer. She began her journey in entertainment on Disney, starring in the television shows "Shake It Up" and "K.C. Undercover". Her most recent and appraised roles are in Euphoria as the main character Rue, The Greatest Showman, Spiderman: Homecoming, and most recently, the film Malcom and Marie. She has also competed in "Dancing with the Stars" and released music independently. She's been on the cover of numerous magazines include Elle, Vogue, and Glamour to name a few.







Early Life

Zendaya Maree Stoermer Coleman was born on September 1, 1996 (making her a Virgo) in Oakland, California. She is the last of five children born to parents Claire and Kazembe Ajamu Coleman. As a child she performed in local theaters, trained in a conservatory program, and attended Oakland School of the Arts.
